What Spot does

Spot helps travelers check a room or rental for hidden cameras and trackers using four tools:

  • Wi-Fi / network scan — lists devices on your network and flags camera-like ones.
  • Infrared camera scan — uses the rear camera to spot the IR LEDs of night-vision cameras.
  • Magnetic sweep — uses the magnetometer to find magnetic signatures of concealed electronics.
  • Tracker sweep — uses Bluetooth to detect item trackers (AirTag, Tile, SmartTag, Chipolo).

Spot is a detection aid and cannot guarantee it will find every hidden device.

Subscription & billing

  • It renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the period ends.
  • Manage or cancel: open the Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Spot.
  • Restore Purchases: available on the Spot paywall if you reinstall or switch devices.
  • Refund requests are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
