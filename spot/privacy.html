Privacy Policy

Spot — Hidden Camera Detector · Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Spot mobile app (“Spot”, the “App”), operated by Glyde Games Oyun ve Yazilim Anonim Sirketi (“we”, “us”), handles information. We built Spot to protect your privacy, so we collect as little as possible.

1. What we do NOT do

  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not use advertising networks, ad SDKs, or the IDFA.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with data brokers.
  • Because we do not engage in tracking, the App does not request App Tracking Transparency.

2. Scanning stays on your device

When you run a scan, all detection happens on your iPhone. The Wi-Fi devices found, camera detections, sensor readings, and any camera streams you view are processed locally and are not uploaded to us.

3. Information we process

  • Anonymous product analytics (via PostHog): aggregated, anonymous usage events such as which features are opened and whether a scan completed, plus basic device/app info and optional session replays of the App’s own interface. Used only to improve the App; not sold and not linked to your identity.
  • Subscription state (via RevenueCat): purchase and entitlement information needed to provide and restore your subscription. Payment is processed by Apple; we never receive your full payment details.

4. Device permissions

  • Local Network — to scan the Wi-Fi you are connected to for camera-like devices. Foreground only; we never scan remote/internet hosts.
  • Camera — for the infrared lens scan.
  • Bluetooth — to detect nearby item trackers (AirTag, Tile, etc.).
  • Location (While Using) — required by iOS only to read the connected Wi-Fi network name. Not used for positioning or location tracking.
  • Motion — for the magnetometer sweep.

5. Data retention

Anonymous analytics are retained only as long as needed to understand product usage. On-device scan data is not stored by us and is cleared as you use the App.

6. Children

Spot is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Your choices

You can disable permissions any time in iOS Settings, and manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID settings.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date.
