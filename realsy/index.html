What Realsy does

Realsy helps you check whether a photo someone sent you is stolen or reused — a common sign of a fake (“catfish”) profile. Upload the photo and Realsy searches the public web for places it appears, then gives you a simple green / yellow / red signal.

Realsy is a signal, not proof: brand-new AI-generated faces and photos from private accounts won't show up anywhere. Always video-call before you trust someone.

Your photo is deleted right after each check. We never build a face database and never sell your data.

Subscription & billing

  • Realsy Pro is an auto-renewable subscription (weekly, monthly, or yearly), billed to your Apple ID. It may include a free trial.
  • It renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the period ends.
  • Manage or cancel: open the Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Realsy.
  • Restore Purchases: available on the Realsy paywall and in Settings if you reinstall or switch devices.
  • Refund requests are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Account & data
